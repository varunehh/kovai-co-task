Model Training & Comparison Lab

Technical performance metrics and backtesting results based on the last 7 days of known data.

Methodology

Backtesting: Models were trained on historical data up to 7 days ago. They then predicted those 7 days "blind".
Evaluation: Predictions were compared to actuals using Mean Absolute Error (MAE) and Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE).

Overall Champion

SARIMA / LightGBM

SARIMA and LightGBM performed best on specific routes. Prophet struggled with the sharp zero-values in recent data.
